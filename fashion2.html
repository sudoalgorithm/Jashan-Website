Rules

This will be an inter-college event where individual designers get to showcase their designs which will be based on a theme of their choice.


Time limit

 10 min per contestant

Judging criteria

 Theme, Costume, Creativity (materials used), design. They will not be judged by their walk.
GENERAL RULES
  • There can be any number of participants from each college.
  • The designers can either wear their own design and walk or they can have another model wearing their design.
  • There will be two rounds; first round will only have the models walking to show the designers work and the second round will have the designer walking with her/his model. They get 5 minutes to speak about their design and then answer any questions posed by the judges.
  • Vulgarity of any kind would lead to disqualification of the contestant from the event.
  • The decision of the judges and organizing committee will be final.
  • Green room will be provided.
  • No props and costumes will be provided by us. Contestants have to bring their own.
