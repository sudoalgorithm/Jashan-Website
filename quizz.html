Jashn 17

Rules

A glorious celebration of India’s most beloved cultural traditions, the Tamasha will focus on Quiz hosts scouring through the crowd in the traditional garb of a village native or a typical city Indian, asking audiences questions. Characters would include famous Indians from television or history. This would be followed by the final event which will test the quizzed students on their mental strengths about anything and everything related to life in India.


No. of Participants

 2 per team

Time limit

 Preliminary Round: 30 minutes + Stage Round: 60 minutes
JUDGING CRITERIA
  • The top six teams will be selected in teams in of two for the stage round depending on the maximum points scored.
  • Based on a system of a buzzer or call, any team that chooses to answer first before the waiting time is over will be credited 15 points for a correct answer and will -10 if it answers wrong.
  • No points will be lost if the team does not pick the question before the waiting time of 10 seconds. Getting the right answer in such a case will credit 10 points.
  • Answering a question that has been passed from a team will grant 5 points to the answering team.
  • All answers will have to be written on a piece of paper and will be reviewed by the quizmaster.
GENERAL RULES
  • Directly shouting out the answers will not be entertained with any points. The question will be of no relevance in the list and will be removed.
  • Entering teams only have a maximum of 10 minutes to finish up the preliminary written round.
  • The quizmaster’s rule is final. All else is to be taken by the associate members.
  • Use of internet and external sources is prohibited and if found will be punished by removal from the competition.
  • Hints to questions can only be given in the situation when all team members agree to do so.
